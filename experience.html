Experience

My Work Force Experience

As I reflect on my professional journey, I'm struck by the diverse experiences that have shaped my career path. At Brigham Young University, I am pursuing a major in Information Systems with a special focus on Entrepreneurship and Technology. This choice was driven by my passion for staying at the forefront of technological advancements and my entrepreneurial spirit.

My work experience commenced at Cost Plus World Market in Woodinville, WA, from November 2017 to February 2018. Here, I delved into the retail environment, mastering the stock system, and learning the nuances of product cycling to maximize sales. This role also honed my customer service skills, as I operated point-of-sale systems and adeptly handled customer inquiries.

From March 2019 to September 2019, I served as an Associate Trainer at Panera Bread in Lynnwood, WA. In this role, I deepened my understanding of product and service offerings and cultivated essential skills like patience and effective communication, especially in fast-paced situations.

A significant shift in my career occurred when I joined the missionary force for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints as a Personal Assistant to the President and Full-Time Representative in Northern Italy, from February 2020 to June 2022. This position not only refined my interpersonal skills through cold-calling and daily interactions but also developed my leadership abilities as I guided and supervised up to 130 full-time representatives.

Following this, I joined Home Depot in Bothell, WA, as a Pro Sales Associate from June 2022 to September 2022. My role involved liaising with business clients, becoming adept in company software, and resolving complex customer inquiries, which required a comprehensive understanding of interdepartmental operations.

Most recently, I began teaching Italian at the MTC, imparting language skills to groups ranging between 6 and 14 students. This teaching experience has been instrumental in enhancing my communication, organization, and pedagogical skills, challenging me to adapt to various learning styles and effectively convey complex information.

Each of these experiences, marked by their respective timelines, has been a stepping stone in my career, equipping me with a versatile skill set and a dynamic approach to professional challenges. As I continue to grow and evolve in my career, I remain dedicated to learning, innovating, and leading with a commitment to excellence and integrity.

  1. Italian Teacher at MTC (December 2022 – Present)
  2. Pro Sales Associate at Home Depot (June 2022 – September 2022)
  3. Full-Time Representative at Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints (February 2020 – June 2022)
  4. Associate Trainer at Panera Bread (March 2019 – September 2019)
  5. Sales Associate at Cost Plus World Market (November 2017 – February 2019)
